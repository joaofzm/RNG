Instructions

Click start to active the alerts asking for your opponents names.

You must fill them up on the right order (check the images bellow for clarification on the right order).
It's VERY important you type "me" (without the commas) once you're filling your own arena, or else the tracker won't work properly.

We reccomend you do it during your load screen, but you can still do it after the game started by following the order of the players on the health bars.




















Once the game started, just click the players you face on the board at the left. Red players are possible opponents, while green players can't be your opponent on the current turn.

When a player is eliminated, click "Eliminate X" to eliminate that player, then continue using it the same way. Worth noting the algoritm resets when a player dies, so all squares will turn red to reflect that.

You can always correct miss clicks with the "undo" button, as well as "Undo elimination x" if you eliminated the wrong player.
